Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a support ticket?

You can submit a support ticket through our online portal by filling out the ticket form with your name, email, issue description, and priority level. Our team will acknowledge your ticket within 2 hours during business hours.

What is the average resolution time?

Resolution time depends on ticket priority. Low-priority issues are typically resolved within 48 hours, medium-priority within 24 hours, and high-priority issues are addressed within 4 hours. Critical issues receive immediate attention.

Can I request a specific technician?

While we cannot guarantee a specific technician, you can add a note in your ticket request if you have worked with someone previously and had a positive experience. We will do our best to accommodate your preference.

What are your support hours?

Our helpdesk is available Monday through Friday, 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM local time. Emergency support for critical system outages is available 24/7. You can submit tickets anytime through our online portal.

Do you offer remote or onsite support?

We offer both remote and onsite support. Remote support is available for most software and configuration issues. Onsite support is typically scheduled for hardware issues, system installations, and network troubleshooting.
