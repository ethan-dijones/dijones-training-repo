Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a support ticket?

You can submit a support ticket by filling out the form above with your name, email, and issue description. Click the "Submit" button and your ticket will be recorded. Our team will respond within 24 hours during business hours.

What is the average resolution time?

Resolution time depends on the priority of your ticket. Low-priority issues are typically resolved within 48 hours, medium-priority within 24 hours, and high-priority issues are addressed within 4 hours. Critical system outages receive immediate attention from our team.

Can I update my ticket after submission?

Yes! You can update your ticket by replying to the confirmation email you receive after submission. Include your ticket ID in your response and any additional details that might help our team resolve the issue faster.

What are your support hours?

Our helpdesk is available Monday through Friday, 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM local time. You can submit tickets anytime through our online form. Emergency support for critical system outages is available 24/7 by calling our emergency line.

Do you offer remote or on-site support?

We offer both remote and on-site support depending on the nature of the issue. Most software and configuration issues are handled remotely for faster resolution. Hardware issues, system installations, and network troubleshooting typically require on-site visits, which can be scheduled at your convenience.
